Privacy Policy for VideoNotes Pro

Effective date: March 7, 2026

Overview

VideoNotes Pro stores your notes locally in your browser using Chrome extension storage. We do not run our own server for note storage and we do not sell personal data.

Data We Store

  • Video note content you write
  • Video title and timestamp metadata
  • Video URL metadata needed for note linking

Where Data Is Stored

Your notes are stored locally on your device using chrome.storage.local.

Payments

Payments and subscription status are handled by ExtensionPay and Stripe. VideoNotes Pro does not directly process or store your payment card details.

Third Parties

We use the following third-party services for billing and licensing:

  • ExtensionPay
  • Stripe

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with advertisers.

Contact
