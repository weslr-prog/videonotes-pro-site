## 2) Long Description (ready to paste)
VideoNotes Pro helps you turn long YouTube videos into structured notes you can actually use.

Capture a timestamp in one click, write your note, and later jump back to the exact second. Keep everything in a focused side panel so your video stays front and center.

Whether you are studying, researching, or documenting tutorials, VideoNotes Pro helps you review faster and remember more.

### What you can do
- Capture timestamped notes while watching YouTube
- Click any note to jump back to the exact moment
- Search notes quickly across your current video or all sessions
- Pin important notes so the most valuable moments stay on top
- Expand the notes area for easier reading and review
- Export notes as Markdown and import them back later

### Why users choose VideoNotes Pro
- Fast workflow with minimal clicks
- Focused interface inside Chrome side panel
- Local-first note storage in your browser
- No account required to start

### Privacy
Your notes are saved locally in Chrome storage on your device.

### Best for
- Students reviewing lectures and tutorials
- Researchers collecting timestamped references
- Professionals extracting highlights from training videos
- Creators saving reusable insights from long-form content
